Scenario

You and a crew of Wright Students have been abducted by aliens in an alien invasion. The aliens are so far advanced that if they truly wanted to, they could take over all of earth in about a day.

However, before they do anything too drastic, they want to test Humans to see if they are worthy of being spared. That's where your group comes in, it is up to all of you to solve the puzzles that the aliens have put in front of you in order to escape and save the rest of Earth.

Are you up to the task?

Puzzle #2

Congratulations if you have made it to the second puzzle. Before you are images from either our own kind or which hold some meaning toward us. Analyze the patterns within each image and find the letter or number that does not belong. Make sure to keep track of uppercase letters as every detail is important to unlock this puzzle.

In puzzles #3-5, all of the codes can be found in the following 3 documents, not all of them however will help you.

For puzzle #4, we have decided to look a little deeper into your lives, we know many of you are studying engineering. As a result, we want you to reflect on the topics that you are learning in and solve a crossword puzzle that will give you the code to unlock the fourth lock.


Lastly, for puzzle #5, We want also want you to reflect a little more. Find the numbers used to Contact what many of you would call a second home. input all 10 numbers into the final lock and if every code lights up green, you have succedded in your tasks.
